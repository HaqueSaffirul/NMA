Meet the studio

Designing immersive mehndi experiences for modern celebrations.

NAYASA Mehndi Artist is a Mumbai-born studio specializing in high-touch mehndi experiences for weddings, festivals, and fashion-forward productions worldwide.

Rooted in tradition, innovating for the future.

Founded by Nayasa Chaturvedi in 2013, our studio grew from a passion project into an award-winning collective of designers, illustrators, and aftercare specialists. We translate lived experiences, heirloom stories, and cultural symbolism into enduring mehndi narratives.

Our artists undergo quarterly certifications in dermatology-approved henna compounds, ergonomic application, and inclusive design practices. With each celebration, we strive to capture emotion, movement, and meaning through mindful artisanal detail.

Studio ethos

Values that guide every stroke.

Intentional Design

Every motif is researched, sketched, and contextualized. Our design library spans 2,400+ original elements, allowing us to build bespoke narratives quickly and responsibly.

Client Care

We offer concierge-level communication, weekly planning updates, and post-event stain monitoring to ensure each client receives attentive support.

Sustainable Impact

From sourcing organic henna leaves directly from Rajasthani farms to using solar-powered cone packaging facilities, sustainability drives our operations.

How we work

Structured process for seamless celebrations.

01

Discovery Workshop

Moodboard collaboration using our digital design suite. We capture cultural references, wardrobe palettes, and key ceremonies to shape the creative direction.

02

Design Blueprint

We deliver sketch previews, stain forecasts, and timeline charts for rehearsals and final application days, ensuring every detail is signed off.

03

On-Site Artistry

Our mobile studio setup includes climate-controlled henna storage, sanitization hubs, and live content capture teams for social and print deliverables.

04

Aftercare & Reunion

Post-event follow-up with stain enhancement tips, product recommendations, and an optional reveal shoot featuring your final stain at peak contrast.
